<objective>
Add the downed/revive/team-broadcast juice and wire the scaffolded-but-unused `GameEvents.player_downed`/`player_revived` signals. This is the roadmap's wave-5 concern: collapse animation, team-visible revive progress ring, team-visible success burst, and team-visible big-hit feedback.

Two confirmed gaps drive the design: (1) `set_revive_progress` is currently a single-target `rpc_id(target_id, ...)` — only the downed player's own client draws the bar, so the ring would be invisible to the rest of the team (COOP-02); the fix widens it to a `call_local` broadcast, which is safe because `Player` nodes are deterministically named `Player_%d`. (2) "Big hit" has no replicated field carrying the hit position, so it needs a small new broadcast (`GameEvents.emit_big_hit(pos)`, D-16) riding the existing significant-hit trigger site (the `from_elite` branch in `Player.receive_damage` that already calls `Game.notify_significant_hit`). Collapse and success ride the already-replicated `is_downed` diff (Pattern A), and the scaffolded `player_downed`/`player_revived` signals are emitted at the host-authoritative sites so they become live.

All team broadcasts target stable-path nodes only (`Game.gd`, `GameEvents` autoload, deterministically-named `Player_%d`) — never `randi()`-named Enemy/Bullet nodes (roadmap pitfall).

Purpose: Make shared co-op moments (downs, revives, big hits) legible to the whole team, per the D-17 whole-room-visible camera reality.
Output: Collapse + team-visible revive ring + success burst + big-hit broadcast; live player_downed/player_revived signals.
</objective>
