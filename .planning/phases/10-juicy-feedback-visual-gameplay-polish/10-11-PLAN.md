<objective>
Add the evolution transform "closure moment" (PROG-03, D-14): a capped (~1-1.5s), non-blocking, multi-sensory transform that reads as a satisfying beat without becoming a cutscene. It composes a ~0.5s charge-up glow/shake build-up on the character, then an element-colored particle burst plus the sprite swap to the new stage. Screen-space personal effects (shake/flash) are gated to the transforming player's own screen (`is_multiplayer_authority()`); the particle burst and sprite swap are visible identically to every peer via the existing `set_evolution_stage` RPC, which already fires on all peers.

Purpose: Make hitting a new stage feel like a real transformation while preserving full agency for everyone.
Output: Charge-up-then-reveal evolution transform hooked into set_evolution_stage, capped and non-blocking.
</objective>
