<objective>
Create the two dedicated audio buses (`Music`, `SFX`, both routed to `Master`) that CONTEXT.md D-09's volume sliders require, and reassign `Sfx.gd`/`Music.gd` off the hard-coded `Master` bus onto them. This is the confirmed Pitfall-7 prerequisite: `Sfx.gd` and `Music.gd` both hard-code `bus = "Master"` today and no `default_bus_layout.tres` exists, so a volume slider calling `AudioServer.set_bus_volume_db` on a non-existent bus would silently no-op. This plan is a zero-dependency, self-contained foundational fix that runs in parallel with the Juice engine (Plan 10-01) and lands before the Settings panel (Plan 10-05) wires the sliders.

Purpose: Make independent Music/SFX volume control possible without per-call multiplier plumbing.
Output: `default_bus_layout.tres` with Master/Music/SFX; `Sfx.gd` on `SFX`; `Music.gd` on `Music`.
</objective>
