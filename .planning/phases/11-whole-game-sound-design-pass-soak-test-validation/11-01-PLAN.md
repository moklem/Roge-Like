- D-05: weapons/pickups/vehicle-y abilities lean into the CARIAD car-part theme (metallic clunks, engine revs, horn honks, servo whirs). Elemental FX and organic effects (Engineer heal sparkle) are NOT forced into the car metaphor.
- D-06: elemental cues echo the existing HUD car-framing — Ice → AC-compressor hiss (matches "AC COLD"), Fire → engine-overheat rattle (matches "ENGINE OVERHEAT"), Earth heal → soft servo/massage-chair hum (matches "SEAT MASSAGE").
- D-07: UI/menu style is Claude's discretion, defaulting to quiet/subtle per Sfx.gd's doc comment ("deliberately quiet... subtle feedback cues, not the focus of the mix").
- D-09: only TWO moments get a Music-layer reaction — evolution transform and boss death / loop-end. Boss phase-change stays SFX-only.
- D-11/D-12 resolution (record in checklist): Spinning Tires gets NO dedicated cue — its audible identity is the shared `hit()` tick already firing on every peer per its 0.5s per-enemy cooldown. Ice trail zone per-enemy slow gets NO dedicated cue — Ice's audible identity is the AC-compressor hiss fired via the "ac" HUD-event echo (onset-gated).
- SFX-02 onset-only: Fire burst and Earth passive heal produce sound only on activation onset. This is achieved by Sfx.gd's HUD-echo ONSET-GATE (Plan 11-02 Task 2), NOT a fixed debounce: the gate advances its per-event last-seen timestamp on EVERY hud_event and only re-fires after a real gap (> the source's tick interval), so Earth's continuous 1.0s "seat_massage" re-emit and Fire's rapid "engine" bursts each collapse to a single onset cue. IMPORTANT: the Earth heal genuinely re-emits "seat_massage" every 1.0s (Game.gd _tick_earth_effects) and the CarHUD relies on that repeat to keep the SEAT MASSAGE indicator lit — so the emit is left untouched at the source and the onset discipline is enforced only in Sfx.gd's listener.
- Engineer passive heal (ROLE-07, Game.gd `_tick_engineer_passive`): distinct heal source from Earth's element heal and the HealDrone pulse. Gets its own onset-only cue `Sfx.engineer_heal()`, wired in Plan 11-03 via a false->true active edge + a small call_local RPC (organic heal sparkle, D-05 not car-themed).
